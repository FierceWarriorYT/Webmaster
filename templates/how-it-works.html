Space-Suits


Cosmic Cruises, the safety and well-being of our customers are of utmost importance to us. When it comes to space travel, the use of spacesuits is essential to ensure the protection of our passengers during their journey. Our spacesuits would be made from the latest and most advanced materials, including high-tech fibers and special coatings that are capable of withstanding the extreme conditions of space. Additionally, each spacesuit would be equipped with a built-in life support system, which would provide the necessary oxygen, temperature control, and communication capabilities to keep our passengers safe and connected throughout their journey. The suits would be designed to be modular, allowing for easy customization depending on the needs and preferences of each individual passenger.


Training


We at Cosmic Cruises provide customers with an unforgettable experience. In order to achieve this, we believe that training customers in the skills and knowledge necessary to live on different planets is essential to achieving this goal. Our training programs would be led by experienced space professionals and incorporate simulations and practical exercises. Customers would learn how to manage resources such as oxygen, water, and energy, as well as basic survival skills such as how to find food and water, build shelter, and navigate in unfamiliar terrain. We believe that our training programs will give customers the skills and knowledge they need to have a safe and enjoyable experience on their journey to different planets.

Vehicles

Space-Shuttle

The Space Shuttle will be equipped with the latest in luxury amenities, including spacious seating areas, private cabins, gourmet dining options, and entertainment systems that provide a range of options for relaxation and entertainment during the journey. The shuttle will also be equipped with advanced life support systems and environmental controls to ensure that our customers are safe and comfortable during their journey through space.

Rover

Our vehicles would be carefully designed to be both functional and safe, taking into account the unique characteristics of each planet's environment, such as surface composition, gravity, and atmospheric conditions. For example, on Mars, we would use rovers specifically designed to navigate the rocky terrain and steep slopes, while on the moon, we would use lunar rovers that can travel over the rough and dusty lunar surface. In addition, our vehicles would be equipped with the necessary equipment to keep our passengers safe and comfortable during their journey, including life support systems, communication systems, and environmental control systems.

Launch & Recovery

Skyhook

To make our trips as cost efficient and as fast as possible, Cosmic Cruises will use skyhooks that have been build on Earth and each of our locations. The aircraft that we have will attach to the skyhook which will use rotational momentum to hurl the spacecraft at much higher speeds than a conventional rocket which would lead to a much faster trip that will not use much of the limited, valuable fuel the spaceships have. Then, the skyhook at the destination will catch the aircraft which will keep restoring the angular momentum of the skyhook that will be used to launch it back to Earth. Bases

On the surface of these destinations, there will be the luxurious Cosmic Cruise Resorts where travelers can spend their time and will be housed and treated to the best facilities. From these bases, tourists can take our rovers to explore many regions and indulge in one of many different activities offered at each of our destinations. This will also serve as a spaceship refueling center and and needed restocking or repairs can be made. This will allow the crew to prepare and consolidate for the long-ride back home, even with the increased speed of the skyhook.
